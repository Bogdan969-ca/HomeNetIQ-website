Privacy Policy

Proudly serving Edmonton — the capital city of Alberta — and surrounding areas

Effective: September 2025

1 Overview

HomeNetIQ (“we”, “us”) provides home networking, device support, and practical AI services for homes and small offices. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your choices. It applies to our website homenetiq.ca and to messages you send us (e.g., email or the contact form).

Next: Information we collect →

2 Information we collect

  • Information you provide — name, email, phone number, and message content when you contact us via the website or by email.
  • Technical information — standard data your browser sends (IP address, user agent, pages viewed). We do not sell or broker personal data.
  • Service providers — we use trusted vendors to operate our site: Form submissions may be processed by Formspree; basic traffic metrics may be measured by Google Analytics.

3 How we use information

  • To respond to inquiries and deliver requested services.
  • To operate, secure, and improve our website and support processes.
  • To communicate with you about scheduling, support, and service updates.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce agreements.

4 Legal bases

Where applicable (e.g., under Canadian and international privacy laws), we process information on one or more of the following bases:

  • Consent — e.g., when you submit our contact form.
  • Contract — to provide services you requested.
  • Legitimate interests — to operate and protect our services (balanced against your rights).
  • Legal obligation — to comply with applicable laws.

5 When we share information

  • Vendors/Service providers — strictly to help us run our website and email (e.g., Formspree for form handling, email providers). They are bound by confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal and safety — if required by law or to protect our rights, users, or the public.
  • We do not sell your personal information.

6 Cookies & tracking

Our site is lightweight and privacy-minded. If enabled, we may use:

  • Essential site cookies — to deliver the site securely.
  • Analytics — Google Analytics may collect aggregated usage metrics (e.g., page views, referrers). You can opt out via your browser’s settings or add-ons from Google.
  • No ads — we don’t run behavioral advertising on this site.

You can control cookies through your browser settings and privacy tools.
